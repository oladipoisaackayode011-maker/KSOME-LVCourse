Our Story

About KSOME

Built on a mission to empower every individual with the digital skills to tell their story and share their purpose with the world.

Isaac Kayode Oladipo

Founder & CEO, KSOME Companies Limited

Isaac Kayode Oladipo is the visionary founder of KSOME Companies Limited — a man whose passion for technology, creativity, and people drives everything KSOME stands for. He is an author, gospel musician, preacher, and web developer dedicated to helping individuals and businesses grow through innovation.

Isaac founded KSOME to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity in digital media. His belief is simple: every person has a story worth telling, and digital skills are the tools that make that story heard.

Under his leadership, KSOME has trained students, content creators, churches, and organizations — transforming lives one skill at a time.
